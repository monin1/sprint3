Lie back on a flat bench. Hold the bar with a normal width grip and hold the bar straight over you.

Description

Description

Preparation:Load your desired weight on a barbell that is on a weight rack, about chest high. Cross your arms and place your hands on top of the barbell while your upper arms are parallel to the floor.

Execution: Squat down by bending your hips back, letting your knees bend forward, keeping your back straight and knees pointed in the same direction as your feet. Descend until thighs are just past parallel to floor. Extend your knees and hips until your legs are straight. Return and repeat.

Description

Preparation:Load your desired weight on a barbell that is on a weight rack, about chest high. Place the barbell on your shoulders with your hands grabbing and supporting it and stand with your feet shoulder width apart.

Execution: Step back with your right leg while bending your supporting left leg. Plant the front of your right foot far back on floor. Lower your body by flexing your knee and hip of your left leg until the knee of your right leg is almost in contact with floor. Return and repeat by alternating lunge with opposite leg.

Description

Preparation:Stand with in front of a loaded barbell and bend your knees and body forward. Grab the bar with an overhand grip with your hands just over shoulder width apart.

Execution: Exhale as you push up and lift the bar with your legs and at the same time bring your torso to an upright position. Once you are standing, push your chest out and bring your shoulder blades back so you are standing upright at attention. Return while keeping your legs straight and make sure the barbell touches the floor before starting your next rep.
